About This Project

This web application is designed to help users track their daily expenses and income efficiently. With an easy-to-use interface, animated transitions, and local data storage, it provides a smooth and productive experience for managing personal or organizational finances.

Users can:

  • Add, edit, and delete financial entries
  • View total income and expense summaries
  • Navigate seamlessly between different modules: Home, Expenses, Income, and About
